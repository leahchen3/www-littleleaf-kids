About Us - Committed To Fun

Little Leaf Daycare (Francis Wood) is a licensed child care center dedicated to nurturing young minds. Our specialized Mandarin-immersion program empowers children to learn the language while fostering critical thinking and problem-solving skills, providing a strong foundation for kindergarten readiness.

Each day is filled with activities, including music, art, story time, games, and outdoor play, designed to inspire creativity and holistic development. We strongly believe in hands-on experiences and encourage self-led exploration, allowing children to actively engage and interact with their learning environment.
